About Us

Professional Staff

The professional consulting staff of The Gables is composed of a medical director, psychologist, consulting psychiatrist, treatment director, licensed chemical dependency counselors, chemical dependency technicians, nurses, dietician, and spirituality consultants. These professionals work cooperatively to continually develop and monitor the individual treatment plan.

The Gables professional kitchen staff provides meals created on site, providing wholesome foods and well-balanced meals.

The Home

The Gables Mansion, built in 1912, in a quiet residential neighborhood, was purchased by the current owner in September 1982. The lovely four-story English Tudor mansion was completely renovated to house The Gables for chemically dependent adult women. The main level has the "great room" and dining room beautifully decorated to promote a serene and harmonious atmosphere. The upper two levels, decorated in quiet blue and white with accents of peach, have numerous bedrooms as well as group rooms, lounges, and counselor offices. The garden level provides additional living areas and bedrooms. The quality of these surroundings lends an atmosphere of warmth and security to the recovery setting.
