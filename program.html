The Program

The Gables offers treatment for women to experience a structured program specifically designed to introduce her to a chemical free life style and the 12-step concept. Treatment is offered so she can live free from consequences caused by her use of alcohol and other drugs.

The High Intensity (Primary), Medium Intensity (Extended Care) and Low Intensity (Half-Way House) treatment programs provide a full-day curriculum of care in a residential setting. All treatment programs are designed for women whose problems related to their disease have not been sufficiently resolved by other treatment experiences. Our highly qualified treatment team develops a comprehensive individualized treatment plan focusing on issues specific to women:

  • Relationships/Co-dependency
  • Mental Health
  • Self-esteem
  • Employment
  • Independent sober living skills development
  • Relapse Prevention
  • Housing
  • Comprehensive discharge plan
  • Dialectical Behavioral Therapy (DBT)
The program is designed to build positive self-esteem, assist in identity development, and create more effective interpersonal relationship skills. By developing an environment that focuses on strengths, women are able to experiment with new behaviors and tools for positive change.

Group Therapy

Group therapy is a primary treatment modality at the Gables. The overall goal of the group process is to provide a supportive and therapeutic environment in which women can explore the impact of the use of mood altering chemicals in all areas of their lives. By actively developing positive skills necessary for quality recovery, clients are able to experience change and rewards.

Individual Sessions

The Gables offers individualized therapy for issues specific to each client.
