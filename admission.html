Admission and Charges

Admissions are arranged on an individual basis and limited to women 18 years of age and older who have experienced consequences as a result of their chemical use.

Our Admission staff coordinate with the client, health care professional, and/or significant others to review appropriateness and determine individual care needs.

Financial arrangements are made in advance. Information regarding current per diem and other fees will be given upon request. The Gables accepts most Insurances, self-pay and Government Funding, including consolidated funding (Rule 25).

Additional Charges

  • History and physical by physician/psychiatrist.
  • Overall psychiatric evaluation process when necessary.
  • Psychological testing.
  • Prescription costs and dental costs.
  • Transportation.
